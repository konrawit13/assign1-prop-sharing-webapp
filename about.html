About the solution

Our solution is designed to help finding a quick housing option based on Australia locations for those who wish for a long-stay or short-stay (as far as the regulation allows) - because having a roof over your head is always better than going without.

As the cost of living goes sky rocket, finding a quality affordable stay that suit your lifestyle should become feasible. We offer Minimal-iconism style comparison between properties such as access to public transportation, pet allowance, smoking allowance, short-stay.

In addition, we also value health and well-being related amentities such as open windows, air conditions, heaters, laundry access, and bidets for Asian-style living experience.

Icon Guide and Legend Dictionary

In case you feel lost in the forest of icons, the explainations are here.

# icon name description
1 🪑 Furnished Whether the property is furnished or not
2 number of tenants number of tenants e.g. 1/3 means there is 1 tenant for the maximum of 3
3 🛏 bed sizing K = king size, Q = Queen size, D = Double size, S = Single size
4 Wifi access Has free Wifi access or not
5 🛁 Bathroom E=ensuite, S or means shared
6 🚿 Bidets Are bidets available? (rather than paper-based toilets which are default)
7 Air conditions Whether the property has Air conditions or not
8 Heaters Whether the property has heaters or not
9 Open windows Whether the property has openable windows or not
10 🫧👕✨ Laudry access Whether the property has been provided with washing machines/dryer machines or commercial laudry sites access
11 🍳 Kitchen or Kitchenette E=ensuite, S or means shared, means there is no any
12 Public transportation access How long does it take on walk to the public transportation stop/station
13 🐱 Pets Wether pets are allowed or not
14 🚬 Smoking Smoking in public is generally prohibited, as well as private area on premises
15 backpacker or short-stay Wether short-stay is permitted or not
